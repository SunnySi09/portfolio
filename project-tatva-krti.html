Tatva Krti Storefront
Shopify E-commerce Revamp
Client
Tatva Krti
Role
Website and UI/UX Designer
Duration
Apr 2025 – Oct 2025
Challenge
Tatva Krti's existing e-commerce storefront suffered from low product discoverability and high mobile checkout drop-offs. User session recordings showed customers dropping off at category navigation stages because item groupings were unclear.
Approach
1. Usability Testing & Session Replays
Analyzed user interactions on Shopify, discovering that layout gaps on mobile devices led to high scroll-fatigue during category checks.
2. Navigation & Hierarchy Redesign
Restructured the primary menu on Figma into visual cards, placing popular catalogs within one-tap range of mobile users.
3. Shopify Storefront Integration
Optimized front-end custom liquid sections and layouts on Shopify to match responsive design system breakpoints.
Solution
We designed a revamped responsive storefront with a clean, visual hierarchy. By restructuring category grids, cleaning up side margins, and standardizing product info cards, browsing comfort improved.
Results
7% increase in overall user engagement
12% increase in checkout success rates
